Your Scenario
A large corporation has N employees each with a computer device (N is an even integer 2 ≤ N ≤ 1,000,000).

Every day all employees exchange 3 messages with three other employees (assume
that the targeted employees are selected at random). One of the employees was not cautious and started spreading an acquired computer virus through their email messages.

Every person who receives the message follows these rules:
1. The likelihood of acquiring and spreading the computer virus to another employee is 40%.
2. After a person receives the virus 2 times, they are alerted and will take action to no longer spread it further.

Your Task
•	For different values of N = {100, 1000, 10000}, run your simulation several times to
determine:
o	On average, what % of the attendees will have heard the rumor after 3 days?
o	On average, what % of the attendees will have heard the rumor after 20 days?
o	On average, what % of the attendees will have heard the rumor after 40 days?
•	 For N= 10,000
o	After how many days will 10% of the employee devices be affected by the virus?
o	After how many days will 50% of the employee devices be affected by the virus?
